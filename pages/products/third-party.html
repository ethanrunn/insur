In the event of an accident, Third Party Motor Insurance covers the insured's liability for the death or injury of a third party and the loss or damage to their property. Third Party Automobile Insurance is one of the mandatory insurance policies. It is the legally required minimum level of automobile insurance for Nigerian road users.

The benefits of the policy, in addition to the fact that it is required by law, make third-party auto insurance a necessity for every Nigerian driver.
