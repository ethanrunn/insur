Accidents can be extremely traumatizing, not only for those directly involved but also for those who are close to them. The victims are shocked and driven into a state of mental and emotional agony when they face the possibility of death, multiple agonizing physical injuries, or permanent disabilities.

We offer a specialized insurance policy known as a "personal accidental insurance policy" to help you and your loved ones financially in the event of such unfortunate accidents in an effort to prevent such fatal outcomes.

This insurance protects against any kind of accident-related loss of life or permanent, partial, or total disability. The insured or their family receives compensation equal to the sum assured coverage in the event of accidental death or permanent disability.
