Burglary Insurance

Insurance that covers losses caused by burglaries. Burglary occurs when a person unlawfully and willfully enters another person's property using force, even if they do not ultimately steal anything.

Comprehensive Motor

Auto insurance that protects you from financial losses caused by risks like accidents, robbery, flooding, fire, etc. Intended to assist you in returning YOUR VEHICLE to the road following any risks.

Fire and Other Perils

You are covered by our fire and other perils policy in case of property damage from fire, lightning, or an explosion of equipment or devices. Protect your company and all of its components.

Goods-In-Transit

This plan is for you if your business involves large-scale road transportation of goods. These kinds of businesses frequently come with high risks because they risk losing valuable goods due to theft, fire, or accident.

Home Owners Insurance

Homeowners and landlords alike will find this plan to be ideal. In addition to replacing your valuables in the event that they are stolen, this insurance plan protects you from the exorbitant costs associated with fire damage and other types of incidents to your home.

Marine Cargo Insurance

Manufacturing and construction companies, importers and any business that imports goods by sea are all eligible to purchase this policy. Businesses that transport goods by sea are protected from perils like storms, sinking, and theft.

Marine Hull Insurance

This policy shields the insured vessel or fleet from physical damage while it is in transit over water. The policy is important because it helps boat owners protect their vehicles from machinery damage and the loss of hull, fittings, liabilities, and other items.

Personal Accident

This protects against any kind of accident-related loss of life or permanent, partial, or total disability. The insured or their family receives compensation equal to the sum assured coverage in the event of death or permanent disability.

Tenant's Insurance

Homeowners aren't the only ones who need insurance. Renters can also safeguard their investment in a smart way. Tenant insurance is sometimes referred to as renter's insurance. Both are synonymous terms that are frequently used interchangeably.

Third-Party Insurance

In the event of an accident, Third Party Motor Insurance covers the insured's liability for the death or injury of a third party and the loss or damage to their property. Third Party Automobile Insurance is the legally required minimum level of automobile insurance for Nigerian road users.
