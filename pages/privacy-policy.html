ABCD Insurance Plc (ABCD) is committed to protecting the privacy of its clients and employees (data subjects) and their personal information. This policy may need to be updated on a regular basis due to changes in data protection and privacy laws, new technologies, or government regulations.



DEFINITIONS OF ROLES


For the purposes of this policy, the roles that are defined are as follows:

The Data Person: is a recognizable individual; one of ABCD's clients, customers, business partners, and employees who can be identified directly or indirectly, particularly by reference to an identification number or one or more factors specific to his physical, physiological, mental, economic, cultural, or social identity.

Administrator of Data: refers to an organization or individual who processes data. The Data Administrator for the purposes of this policy is ABCD Insurance Plc.

Controller of Data: means a person who, either on their own, in conjunction with other people, in common with other people, or as a statutory body, decides the purposes and means of processing personal data. The Managing Director, or whoever he appoints, is the Data Controller for the purposes of this policy.

Administrator of the Data: is appointed by the data controller to ensure compliance with the data protection policy and applicable laws in the strategy and execution of data protection requirements. in accordance with this policy.

The Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (2019) lays out the roles of the Data Administrator, Data Controller, and Data Protection Officer in detail.
