The Whistle Blowing Policy

ABCD Insurance Limited sets the highest standards of conduct for its employees in an effort to eliminate unethical behavior and unfair practices.


We are committed to establishing a culture of honesty, openness, transparency, and compliance in accordance with the company's values and ethics.

It also provides a framework for employees and other stakeholders to report their concerns both internally and externally.


If the following conditions are met, ABCD will ensure that a whistleblower who makes a report will not be victimized or discriminated against because of the report:

a. The report is made by the whistleblower in good faith.
b. The report is reasonable from the whistleblower's perspective.
c. The report is made on the sensible conviction that it is valid.
d. Except when a reward is required by law, the report is not made for personal gain.
e. The policy is followed when making the law.


We will ensure that any and all whistleblower complaints made in accordance with this policy are evaluated and, if necessary, investigated. Any of the following channels can also be used to make internal whistle blowing reports to the company: 234 12345678, whistleblowing@abcdinsurance.com
